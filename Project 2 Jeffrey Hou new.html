Project 2¶

For this project, I decided to use the Kaggle dataset for Life Expectancy Data from World Health Organization. This Dataset contains many statstics about a country's lifestyle as well as their disease statstics. There are 193 countries represented in this dataset. There are 2938 rows and 22 columns. Some questions I have interested in is:

  1. What are the countries with the lowest Polio Immunization rates?
  2. Out of Measles, Polio and Diphtheria, which disease has the most normal distribution when compared to Immunization Percentage?
  3. Is there any correlation between GDP and Expenditure %?
